## Why Every Creative Should Learn 3D (Even Just a Little)

3D can feel intimidating at first: too many buttons, too many views, too many ways to break something. But once I took my first steps into Blender, I realised how powerful even the simplest techniques can be.

Learning 3D expands the way you think. Suddenly, lighting becomes a storytelling tool. Materials behave like real objects. And you’re no longer limited by what a camera can capture — you can create anything you imagine.

You don’t need to master sculpting or complex simulations. Even basic shapes, simple animations or stylised renders can elevate a project. For designers, filmmakers and creatives of any kind, 3D is no longer optional — it’s an exciting playground filled with possibilities.
